Home


You feel a cool breeze and reach for your blanket, your hand meets a damp rough surface. You retract quickly, eyes shooting open as you feel the grainy substance clinging to your hands.

As your eyes adjust you see green and shadow and realize that you are lying in the dirt beneath the canopy of an unfamiliar forest. Where are you? How did you get here? No, how do you get home? You gather the few items of yours that are strewn about, your keys, a tin of mint, and a water bottle that to your surprise is completely full.

You stand up, take in your surroundings, and see the edge of a red cabin peaking between the trees to the right. To your left you see a river. Which way do you go?
